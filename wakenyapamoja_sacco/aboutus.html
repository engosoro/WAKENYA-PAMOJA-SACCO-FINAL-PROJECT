About Wakenya Pamoja Sacco

Wakenya Pamoja Sacco is a leading financial cooperative dedicated to empowering members through savings, credit, and investment opportunities. Established with a vision to promote financial independence, we provide tailored financial solutions to individuals, businesses, and groups.

Our Mission

To provide innovative and accessible financial services that uplift our members and communities.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and member-focused Sacco, fostering economic growth and financial security.

Our Core Values

  • Integrity
  • Transparency
  • Member-Centric Services
  • Innovation
  • Community Empowerment
